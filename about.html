About us

History of MPJ Tailoring Inc.

The business began with humble beginnings. Mr. Jun Pastrana, the owner, started as a tailor under a big Tailoring company owned by a Chinese businessman. He started working at the said company at the year 1982. He began learning his skills as a tailor from his fellow tailors. He saved enough for him to buy his own Sewing Machine. By the year 1994 he left the company and started to build his own. By using the first sewing machine he saved for MPJ Custom Tailor came to be. MPJ simply means the owner's name. Martin Pastrana Jr. Now the business which strarted from just one sewing machine now showcases their work to different respected companies, politicians and even celebrities. Like my father said it does not matter what you have as long as you have enough determination and hardwork, everything will be in place.
